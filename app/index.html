Tom & Dave have one of the largest stock music libraries in all of North America and we’re proud of the fact our music comes from around the world.


We also have a HUGE network of composers that we work with on a daily basis also around the world. Because of the years of working on albums and film and television we have assembled one of the most potent teams in the business. There isn’t a style or project that we can’t tackle.


Sound design is another one of our specialties, again because of all our work in film and TV has us aligned with the top sound designers in North America.


Music Licensing is also something we do regularly. We placed literally thousands of music tracks on television shows, movies and advertising. We have exceptional relationships with the publishing companies and record labels.
